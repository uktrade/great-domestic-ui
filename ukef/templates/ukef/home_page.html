What we offer

We activate the UK supply chain, connecting overseas project sponsors and buyers directly with UK exporters. We offer government-backed financing and insurance, cooperating with investment banks and other financial institutions to offer bespoke solutions on a project-by-project basis.

UK Export Finance cooperates with many other export credit agencies (ECAs), allowing us to collaboratively support major international projects.

How we can help you

We work with UK exporters and overseas buyers - anyone who’s spotted an international business opportunity involving a UK-based company.

  1. Exploring financing options
    If your bank won’t lend, or their rates are too high, we’ll work with you both to explore options.
  2. Examining eligibility
    Once you’ve created a detailed proposal, we’ll examine it in detail: we consider the project itself, but also the ‘risk profile’ of the country you want to export to.
  3. Negotiating terms
    We’ll work with your bank to agree the terms of the deal: this can take some time, especially with complex projects.
  4. Securing finance
    You’ll then be able to access UKEF’s support.
